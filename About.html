Our Story

Established in 1979 by the late Jerry Dotterweich, Jerry D's Saloon has been a cornerstone of the Parkville community for over four decades. Jerry, a Korean War veteran and entrepreneur, envisioned a place where locals could gather for hearty meals, cold drinks, and warm camaraderie. His legacy lives on through the saloon's commitment to quality food and friendly service.

What We Offer

At Jerry D's, we pride ourselves on serving generous portions of classic American fare at reasonable prices. Our menu features favorites like Maryland-style crab cakes, fish & chips, and our signature meatloaf with real mashed potatoes. Whether you're here for brunch, lunch, or dinner, our diverse offerings cater to all tastes.

Community & Atmosphere

More than just a restaurant, Jerry D's Saloon is a community hub. Our cozy, tavern-style setting is perfect for catching up with friends, watching the game, or enjoying live music events. We offer a welcoming environment where everyone feels like family.

Visit Us

Located at 7808 Harford Rd, Parkville, MD 21234, Jerry D's Saloon is open daily to serve you. Come experience the tradition, taste, and hospitality that have made us a beloved fixture in the community.
